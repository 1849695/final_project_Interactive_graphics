Explore

Wondering how to explore my project?

1. Feel free to click and drag across the canvas to view every angle of the teapot.
2. Pitch to zoom (or scroll mousewheel) for a closer look at the reflective surface.
3. Use your arrow keys to translate the teapot across the Catmull Gallery scene.
